This repo houses the Bits n Bio Compendium Website

File structure:


```
.
├── articles/     
├── docs/
├── img/
├── _quarto.yml     
├── about.qmd       
├── articles.qmd 
├── contribute.qmd 
├── index.qmd 
├── LICENSE   
├── README.md       
└── styles.css      
```

| No | Name | Details 
|----|------------|-------|
| 1  | articles/ | Final article documents. One .qmd per article
| 2  | docs/ | Quarto document rendering destination - Source for website
| 3  | img/ | Images used in articles
| 4  | _quarto.yml | Quarto configurations YAML
| 5  | about.qmd | About Team page
| 6  | articles.qmd | Articles directory page
| 7  | contribute.qmd | Contribute page
| 8  | index.qmd | Webssite homepage
| 10 | LICENSE | Licensing agreement
| 11 | README.md | This file
| 12 | styles.css | Document rendering settings
